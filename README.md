# 🏨 Hotel Demand Analytics & Revenue Optimization

## 📌 Project Overview
This project is an end-to-end hotel analytics solution that focuses on understanding demand patterns, pricing strategies, cancellations, and customer behavior.

Using **Python, MySQL, and Power BI**, the project analyzes **119K+ hotel booking records** and delivers actionable insights through a **5-page interactive dashboard** for revenue optimization and business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problems Addressed

- Inaccurate demand forecasting leading to over/under-staffing  
- Suboptimal pricing strategies reducing revenue potential  
- High cancellation rates causing revenue leakage  
- Inability to identify high-value customer segments  
- Poor visibility into seasonal and geographic demand patterns  

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

- ADR (Average Daily Rate)  
- Occupancy Rate  
- RevPAR (Revenue per Available Room)  
- Cancellation Rate  
- Lead Time  
- Revenue (calculated proxy using ADR)  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- **Python** → Data cleaning, feature engineering, EDA  
- **MySQL** → KPI analysis, business queries  
- **Power BI** → Dashboard & visualization  
- **Excel / CSV** → Data source
